About Pasta Amore!

Meet the Chef!

Our head Chef has studied the culinary arts of italian cuisne while studying abroad in Rome! In his time in Rome, our head chef, John Doe, learned the many techniques of the locals. He even puts his own twist on our menu items which make them completly unique compared to others!

The History of Pasta Amore

Pasta Amore was started by our very own head chef's grandfather in 1957. Since then the traditions have been passed down throughout the generations. Our values and goals are deeply rooted in family traditions. Here everyone is treated like family and you can find a friendly atmosphere to enjoy a freshly homestyle meal.

Our Vision

Pasta Amore combines the cooking traditions and ingredients of Italy with creativty, providing a modern twist on timeless classics. Our goal at Pasta Amore is to provide authentic Italian meals at an affordable price so that everyone can enjoy the taste of Italy!
